Since 2013

Elegant Living Starts at Home

The Noleen Homeware began in Pinetown with a simple belief: every home deserves to feel as beautiful as it does functional. What started as a small curtain-making service has grown into a trusted destination for curtains, cushions, bedding and home décor across Durban.

Today, we combine quality fabrics, thoughtful design and professional installation to help hundreds of families turn ordinary rooms into spaces they love coming home to.
