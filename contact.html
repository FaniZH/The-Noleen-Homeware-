Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free measuring and quotes?
Yes — we offer free, no-obligation measuring and quotes for curtains and rails anywhere in Pinetown and greater Durban.
How long does installation take?
Most standard installations are completed within 1–2 hours. Automated curtain systems may take slightly longer depending on complexity.
Can I order custom sizes or fabrics?
Absolutely. Most of our curtains, cushions and bedding can be made to measure or in a fabric of your choice — just ask us on WhatsApp.
What areas do you deliver and install in?
We proudly serve Pinetown and greater Durban, including Kloof, Hillcrest, Durban North and the surrounding areas.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EFT, card payment and cash on installation day. Orders placed online are confirmed and paid via WhatsApp or EFT before dispatch.
